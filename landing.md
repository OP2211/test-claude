
# 🏟️ FanGround — Landing Page Build Instructions

## 🎯 Objective
Build a high-conversion landing page that:
- Instantly resonates with live sports fans
- Communicates emotional value (not features first)
- Drives users to join before or during a match

---

## 🧠 Core Principle

> FanGround is not a product.  
> It is a **live emotional experience**.

Design for:
- Emotion > Information
- Speed > Depth
- Energy > Clean minimalism

---

## 👤 Target Audience (ICP)

- Age: 18–32
- Geography: India (mobile-first users)
- Behavior:
  - Watches IPL / EPL live
  - Uses Twitter / WhatsApp during matches
  - Enjoys banter, rivalry, predictions

---

## 🎨 Design Guidelines

### 1. Theme
- Dark mode default
- High contrast
- Stadium / night match feel

### 2. Colors
- Use **team-based colors (red, blue, yellow)** as accents
- Avoid decorative colors — every color must signal identity

### 3. Typography
- Bold, punchy headings
- Short sentences
- Avoid long paragraphs

### 4. Motion
- Subtle animations:
  - Pulsing red dot (live)
  - Message flow simulation
  - Hover interactions

---

## 📱 Layout Structure (Top to Bottom)

---

### 🔥 1. Hero Section (Above the Fold)

#### Goal:
Immediate emotional hook

#### Content:
- Headline:
  "Stop Watching Matches Alone."

- Subheadline:
  "This is where fans come to feel the game."

- Supporting line:
  "Live banter. Rival fans. Real-time reactions."

#### CTA:
- Primary: "Enter FanGround"
- Secondary: "See Live Match Rooms"

#### Visual:
- Stadium crowd OR live fan reactions
- Optional: chat overlay mock

---

### ⚡ 2. Social Proof Strip

#### Goal:
Build instant credibility

#### Content:
- "90%+ fans use second screen"
- "79% discuss matches live"
- "Now there's a place built for it"

#### Style:
- Horizontal strip
- Minimal icons

---

### 😤 3. Problem Section

#### Goal:
Create relatability

#### Layout:
Grid or stacked cards

#### Content:
- Twitter → chaos
- WhatsApp → no rivalry
- Reddit → too slow
- Alone → no shared energy

#### Closing Line:
"You’re reacting… but no one’s reacting with you."

---

### 🚀 4. Solution Section

#### Goal:
Position FanGround clearly

#### Content:
- Live match rooms
- Fans from both teams
- Color-coded messages
- Real-time reactions

#### Highlight Line:
"When a goal happens — the room explodes."

---

### 🎯 5. Core Features / Experience

Split into 3 blocks:

---

#### 5.1 Fan Identity

- Pick your team once
- Permanent identity
- Color-coded messages

**Line:**
"You’re not anonymous. You represent your team."

---

#### 5.2 Match Rooms

- Pre-match → live → post-match
- Real-time chat
- Active fan count

**Line:**
"This isn’t a feed. It’s a crowd."

---

#### 5.3 Predictions

- Match outcome
- Scoreline
- First scorer

**Line:**
"Prove you knew it."

---

### 🔥 6. Emotional Section (Critical)

#### Goal:
Create desire

#### Content:
Scenario storytelling:

- 300 fans
- Last minute / last over
- Goal / wicket
- Room explodes

#### Closing:
"You’re not watching anymore.  
You’re inside the moment."

---

### 🧠 7. Comparison Section

#### Table:

| Others | FanGround |
|--------|----------|
| Passive | Active |
| No identity | Team identity |
| Fragmented | Match-based |
| Slow | Real-time |
| Alone | Together |

---

### 🎯 8. Audience Qualification

#### Include:
- IPL / EPL fans
- People who argue, react, predict

#### Exclude:
- Casual viewers
- News readers

**Line:**
"For real fans only."

---

### 🚫 9. What It’s Not

- Not scores app
- Not news app
- Not fantasy app

---

### 🚀 10. Final CTA Section

#### Goal:
Drive urgency

#### Content:
- "The next match is coming."
- "Don’t watch it alone."

#### CTA:
- "Join the Next Match Room"
- "Start Your Fan Identity"

---

## ⚙️ Technical Recommendations

### Frontend
- Framework: Next.js / React
- Styling: TailwindCSS
- Animations: Framer Motion

### Performance
- Lazy load images
- Optimize for 3G
- Keep bundle lightweight

### Mobile
- Design mobile-first
- Thumb-friendly buttons
- Sticky CTA

---

## 🧪 Conversion Optimization

### Must Have:
- CTA above the fold
- CTA after every 2 sections
- Sticky bottom CTA (mobile)

### Microcopy:
- Use action verbs:
  - "Enter"
  - "Join"
  - "Feel"
  - "React"

---

## ❌ Avoid

- Long paragraphs
- Technical explanations
- Feature overload
- Generic sports imagery

---

## ✅ Success Criteria

Landing page should make user feel:

- "This is exactly what I do during matches"
- "I need to try this for the next match"
- "This feels different from everything else"

---

## 🧩 Final Tagline

FanGround — Where Fans Feel the Match
